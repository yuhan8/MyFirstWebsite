Easy Sign-up Tools for Student

Designed for the clue center at University of Washington

Quicker sign up.
Easier collaboration.

I designed a web app that improves the sign up process and finds matches for study buddies at the study center.

It's too crowded

Lots of students taking intro math class do their homework and get help from the study center. After talking with the director and interviewed a couple of students at the study center, we found 2 problems.

Crowded and noisy. It's always very crowded and noisy at the study center. Tutors sometimes skip students' names from the paper sign up sheet due to bad handwriting or the noise.

Lack of collaboration. Often, students only work with people they knew. Although collaboration is highly encouraged, not everyone can find a study buddy easily.

Sign Up Go Paperless

All the math homework are based online, so all students bring their laptop to the study center. This allows us to design a web tool on which students can get in the tutor line.

With this new tool, students don't need to get up and sign in on paper. Tutors can read off the digital sign-up sheet and not worry about not being able to understand students' handwriting.

Collaboration Made Easy

Students with the same questions will be grouped together, so that they can work together while waiting for the tutor.

Project In Process

This project is still being working on right now. Most of the results, including the work flow, user interfaces and others, are not definite yet. I will upload the final deliverable and process blog once it is finished.
